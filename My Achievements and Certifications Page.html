Runner Up in LetsHack Leicester 24/25 Hackathon

I had the privilege of securing the runner-up position in the LetsHack Leicester 24/25 Hackathon.

Our team developed a dynamic location-based event tracker website, utilizing Python and JavaScript for the backend and HTML/CSS for the front-end.

As the primary backend developer, I leveraged the pandas library for efficient data manipulation and utilized folium to integrate interactive mapping features that enhanced the user experience.

In addition to my technical contributions, I played a pivotal role in delivering a compelling three-minute pitch, clearly articulating the value of our solution and outlining its potential for future enhancements.
